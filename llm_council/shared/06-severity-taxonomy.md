# Severity Taxonomy

## critical

A likely ship blocker. Could cause serious security/privacy harm, data loss, auth breakage, unrecoverable user harm, or major release failure.

## high

A major issue that should normally be fixed before merge or release. Significant product, reliability, security, or architectural risk.

## medium

A meaningful issue that should be addressed soon. Not necessarily a blocker, but leaving it in place creates real cost or drift.

## low

A legitimate improvement, but not urgent.

## note

Informational guidance or routing signal, not an actual defect.
